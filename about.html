Hello...

I am an entrepreneur who had set up a self-service sushi bar in Christchurch and a mushroom growing business supplying to local cafes, restaurants, and markets.

I've always wanted to become a developer ever since high school. Back then I thought you need to be good at maths to become a programmer, I was terrible at maths and the worst at many other subjects so I didn't pursue that dream.

During the period of running my own business for 8 years, I have found myself drawn back to programming and more specifically web development. The skills I have gained from studying Diploma in Photography have helped me thrive in the design aspect of developing a website using Squarespace which further enhanced my interest to pursue this career. I enjoy problem solving even if it takes me to early hours of the morning to find the right solution.

Was tired of the physical, high pressure, long hours and no social life.

All of the above made me realise I am more capable than I give myself credit and decided to pursue my developer dream. Being apart of digital innovation is exciting, the constant learning is what I live for!

I enjoy spending time with my wife and my two amazing hyperactive dogs. Our dogs (border collies) are a huge part of our life and we love them to bits!
